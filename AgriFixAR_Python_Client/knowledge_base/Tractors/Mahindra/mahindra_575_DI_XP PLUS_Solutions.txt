🟦 PROBLEM 1 — ENGINE DOES NOT START
------------------------------------------
🔹 Chunk 1 — Problem Overview
PROBLEM: Engine does not start
TAGS: not starting, no start, only click, engine not cranking, cranking slow, battery weak
SYMPTOM: Tractor does not turn, or turns very slowly when key is turned.
CAUSES: Weak battery, loose battery wire, fuel not reaching engine, stop knob in OFF.
VISUAL: Starter_Motor, Battery_Terminal

🔹 Chunk 2 — Check Gear & Clutch
PROBLEM: Engine does not start
TAGS: tractor won't start, safety switch, clutch neutral issue
SYMPTOM: No crank until clutch is pressed.
STEPS: Keep gear in neutral; press clutch fully; try starting again.
WARNINGS: Keep area clear before cranking.
VISUAL: Gear_Lever, Clutch_Pedal

🔹 Chunk 3 — Battery Condition Check
PROBLEM: Engine does not start
TAGS: battery weak, low voltage, dim lights, no crank
SYMPTOM: Clicking sound or slow cranking.
STEPS: Open bonnet; locate battery; check for corrosion or loose clamps.
TOOLS: 12mm spanner, dry cloth
VISUAL: Battery_Location, Battery_Terminal

🔹 Chunk 4 — Battery Cleaning & Tightening
PROBLEM: Engine does not start
TAGS: battery cleaning, loose terminals, corrosion, no crank
STEPS: Disconnect negative terminal first; clean posts with brush; reconnect positive then negative; tighten clamps.
TOOLS: 12mm spanner, brush
WARNINGS: Disconnect battery before cleaning.
VISUAL: Battery_Cleaning

🔹 Chunk 5 — Check Fuel Level
PROBLEM: Engine does not start
TAGS: empty tank, fuel low, fuel blocked, diesel not reaching
SYMPTOM: Tractor cranks but won't fire.
STEPS: Open fuel cap; use stick to check level; refill diesel.
TOOLS: Stick, torch
VISUAL: Fuel_Tank

🔹 Chunk 6 — Fuel Shut-Off Knob Check
PROBLEM: Engine does not start
TAGS: stop knob, fuel knob OFF, stop lever, fuel shut-off engaged
SYMPTOM: Engine cranks but no firing.
STEPS: Ensure stop knob is in RUN/ON.
VISUAL: Stop_Knob

🔹 Chunk 7 — Air in Diesel Line (Bleeding Filter)
PROBLEM: Engine does not start
TAGS: air in fuel, bleeding diesel, no firing, after filter change
SYMPTOM: Cranks but does not fire.
STEPS: Loosen bleed screw; pump hand primer until bubble-free fuel flows; tighten screw.
TOOLS: 10–12mm spanner, cloth
VISUAL: Fuel_Filter_Bleed_Screw, Hand_Priming_Pump

🔹 Chunk 8 — Bleeding Injection Pump
PROBLEM: Engine does not start
TAGS: injection pump bleeding, air lock, no fuel at injectors
STEPS: Loosen bleed screw on injection pump; prime until clear fuel flows; tighten.
TOOLS: 12mm spanner
VISUAL: Injection_Pump

🔹 Chunk 9 — Crack Injector Pipe
PROBLEM: Engine does not start
TAGS: injector bleeding, single cylinder bleed, no firing
STEPS: Loosen injector pipe nut; crank 5–10 seconds; tighten; start tractor.
TOOLS: Spanner
VISUAL: Injector_Pipe
WARNINGS: Do not place hands near injector spray.

🔹 Chunk 10 — Starter Motor Click Fix
PROBLEM: Engine does not start
TAGS: starter click, solenoid click, starter jam
SYMPTOM: Only click sound heard.
STEPS: Tap starter body lightly with wooden stick; retry starting.
WARNINGS: Never hit with metal.
VISUAL: Starter_Motor
